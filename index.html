measure4measure

...is a music-based endless runner that pits you against an army of rests. Hone your piano skills as you play your way through an unforgiving barrage of enemies that require a musician’s precision to survive!


Press Release

April 23, 2015 - Medford, MA - Ourmenian Gaming, LLC is pleased to announce their first game, “measure4measure”, coming to PC / Mac.

Now I know that you’re excited, but could you please keep quiet!? Beethoven is composing and he gets reeeeeeeal upset when he’s interru--ohgodno. I think he heard us! Quickly, we need you to guide us to safety!! Navigate your way through the mad composer’s barrage of rests as he furiously attempts to silence you… FOR GOOD.

This is no ordinary endless runner. You must familiarize yourself with Beethoven’s native language: music. As the player, keep your notes alive as long as possible by “playing” your way through the score using a piano-esque control scheme. Collect sharps to stay alive! Gather dynamics to recruit allies to your team and blow Beethoven’s evil rests right off the page!

Meet the Team

Ourmenian Gaming is an independent game development company comprised of four Tufts students: Brian Williamson, Artoun Festekjian, Tyler Beardsley, and Dorian Pistilli. The release of measure4measure will mark their first published game, and buzz is already being generated!
